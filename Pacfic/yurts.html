The Yurts at Pacifc Trails Resort

What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent strcuures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and roof dome that can be opened

How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-sized bed with a down quilt and gas-fired stove. The luxury camping expereince also includes electrcity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should I bring?
Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing.
